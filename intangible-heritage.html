Intangible Heritage Elements

Arabic Coffee and Falconry

Arabic coffee is considered a symbol of generosity and hospitality in Saudi culture, and was included in UNESCO's list of intangible cultural heritage in 2015.

Falconry has also been registered as a living human heritage, practiced in the Kingdom for centuries and considered an authentic part of Bedouin heritage.

Other intangible elements include Najdi Ardah dance, Asiri Cat art, Hijazi Mizmar, and other folk arts.
