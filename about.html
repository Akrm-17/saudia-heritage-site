About Our Mission

Our Heritage, Our Pride

The Saudi Heritage Commission

The Saudi Heritage Commission is dedicated to the preservation, documentation, and promotion of Saudi Arabia's rich cultural and historical heritage. Our mission spans thousands of years of civilization, encompassing archaeological sites, intangible cultural practices, and contemporary cultural expressions.

We work tirelessly to ensure that the treasures of our past are protected for future generations, while simultaneously celebrating the living traditions that continue to define our national identity.

Our Mission

To safeguard and promote Saudi Arabia's cultural heritage through:

  • Preservation: Protecting archaeological sites and cultural artifacts from deterioration and loss.
  • Documentation: Creating comprehensive records of our heritage for research and education.
  • Education: Raising awareness about the importance of cultural preservation among all segments of society.
  • Innovation: Utilizing modern technology to enhance accessibility and understanding of our heritage.
  • International Cooperation: Collaborating with global institutions to share knowledge and best practices.

Our Vision

We envision a Saudi Arabia where cultural heritage is recognized as a cornerstone of national identity, where archaeological discoveries continue to illuminate our past, and where traditional practices remain vibrant and meaningful in contemporary society.

Through our efforts, we aim to position Saudi Arabia as a global leader in heritage preservation and cultural stewardship, demonstrating the value of protecting our shared human history.
